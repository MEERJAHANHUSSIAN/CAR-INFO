ROLLS ROYCE PHANTOM

ENGINE

TThe Rolls-Royce Phantom is equipped with a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine that produces around 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque. It’s designed to deliver smooth, near-silent acceleration with effortless power delivery, staying true to the brand’s signature "waftability" — where the engine feels like it glides rather than accelerates.

WHEEL & TIRES

The Phantom features large, polished alloy wheels—typically 21 or 22 inches—paired with specially designed, noise-reducing tires. These tires include a layer of foam inside to minimize road noise, enhancing the quiet, serene cabin experience that Rolls-Royce is known for.

STEERING

The car uses an electrically assisted power steering system that provides light, effortless turning at low speeds and firm, confident handling at high speeds. The Phantom also includes rear-wheel steering, which allows the rear wheels to turn slightly to improve agility in tight spaces and increase stability on the highway.

SEATS

Inside the Phantom, every seat is a masterpiece of luxury, offering full customization in terms of materials, stitching, and design. All seats come with features like heating, cooling, massage functions, and memory settings, ensuring maximum comfort for every passenger, especially in the rear where executive-class seating is available.

BRAKES

The Phantom is fitted with large ventilated disc brakes and advanced braking technology, including ABS (anti-lock braking system), electronic brake-force distribution, and regenerative braking. These ensure that despite its size and weight, the Phantom stops smoothly and safely, with minimal noise or vibration.

DRIVING TECHNOLOGY

The Phantom incorporates cutting-edge driving technologies such as adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, a head-up display, and night vision assistance. One standout feature is the "Flagbearer" system, which uses cameras to scan the road ahead and adjust the suspension preemptively, working with the air suspension system to deliver the famously smooth “Magic Carpet Ride.”
